About Me

Hello! I’m Sarshij Karn. I'm not just an Electronics + Communication + Information Engineering student — I'm also a part-time philosopher & a tech wizard who accidentally became a vibe coder, trader & a content creator. My brain runs on curiosity, my heart runs on scars, and my project runs on late nights and emotional flashbacks.

I code like it's therapy, trade like it's art, and YouTube like it's war.

"If it’s chaotic, creative, and a bit too ambitious, it’s probably mine." 🔥


Let's Connect.👉👈


💬 Hobbies? More like side quests.

Reading Books

I dive into books like they’re portals — sometimes for knowledge, sometimes just to escape people. From science to self-help, I read it all… because therapy is expensive.

Sports

I play football and cricket not just for fitness, but to occasionally pretend I have balance in life. Sports keep me moving, breathing, and distracted from debugging till 2 AM.

Tech Geek

I keep up with tech like it’s the latest gossip. Gadgets, software, AI — if it blinks, beeps, or updates at midnight, I’m probably already obsessed.

Content Creation

Why just experience life when you can turn it into content? I share what I know, what I build, and sometimes just what broke — with a sprinkle of “watch this fail successfully".

Exploration

New places, new people, new problems to overthink.I explore places like I explore tech — with excitement, mild confusion, and a tendency to get lost. Whether it’s a random street or a new concept, I’m in it for the experience, the chaos, and the story.

Electronics

As an engineering student, I mess with circuits and microcontrollers like a mad scientist. If it sparks, blinks, or makes no sense at first — I’m in.

Qualifications: Yes, I Actually Did That!

4.0 GPA in Class 10

Nailed my Class 10 with a perfect GPA because I like to set the bar high — all thanks, HappyLand Secondary School, for making me look good.

89% in Class 12

Class 12 was a breeze (for me, not the textbooks). Scored 89% at Liverpool International College, proving that hard work and late nights last moment study can make magic happen.

Cracked Engineering Entrance in First Attempt

Didn’t need a backup plan. Cracked the engineering entrance exam on my first try and landed a full scholarship at IOE, TU — because who doesn’t like a challenge?

Pursuing Bachelor's in Electronics, Communication & Information Engineering

Currently chasing knowledge (and an endless supply of coffee) while pursuing my Bachelor's at the National College of Engineering. Electronics, communication, and information — I’ve got it covered.
